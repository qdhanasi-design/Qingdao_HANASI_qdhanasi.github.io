Our Expertise

Who we Are

Qingdao Hanasi Environment Equipment Co., Ltd. was established in 2002. It is a technologically advanced modern manufacturing enterprise that integrates research and development, design, manufacturing, maintenance, and sales of environmental protection equipment. The company provides professional engineering consulting, solution design, equipment manufacturing, maintenance, and modification services to enterprises.

What we do

In October 2013, we received the project approval certificate issued by the Innovation Fund Management Center of the Ministry of Science and Technology for small and medium-sized technology-based enterprises. On December 12, 2013, we obtained the High-tech Enterprise Certificate jointly issued by the Qingdao Science and Technology Bureau, Qingdao Finance Bureau, Shandong Provincial State Taxation Bureau, and Qingdao Local Taxation Bureau. In accordance with the national "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" policy requirements, the company has independently developed and designed a wet, rapid flow electrostatic precipitator. This equipment effectively treats PM2.5, aerosols, and gas transparency in the low-concentration flue gas emissions of large enterprises, ensuring compliance with the environmental standards set by the national "Twelfth Five-Year Plan." The product has passed the appraisal of the Qingdao Science and Technology Committee and is at a leading level domestically.

Our Approach

The company has always adhered to the core of corporate R&D, strengthened close cooperation with research institutes and universities, actively promoted industry-university-research integration, and has obtained multiple national authorized patents.
